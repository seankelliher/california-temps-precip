Heat & Precipitation in California

Is California becoming hotter and drier? These charts look at six cities over the past 50 years, 1970 - 2020. The first chart displays the number of days each year the temperature reached or exceeded 90 degrees Fahrenheit. The second chart displays the annual precipitation in inches. Tap a city's name to hide/show its data.

Data coming from NOAA. This may take a few seconds.

Data for these charts is from the National Centers for Environmental Information, part of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA). These six cities were chosen because they are located in different regions of California and offered data for the entire 50-year span.
